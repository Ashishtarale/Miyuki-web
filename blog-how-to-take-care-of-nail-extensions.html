How to make sure my nail extensions last longer?

Congrats on your fabulous nail extensions! But remember, without proper care, they can chip or pop off at the worst moments. So, how do you keep those stunning nails flawless for weeks? Let’s explore some tips.

1. Take Care of your nail extensions

Treat your nail extensions with care, as they are delicate and require a bit of special attention. Avoid using them for tasks like opening cans, peeling stickers, or removing labels. Remember, your nails are beautiful accessories, not tools.

2. Choose the right length and shape

When selecting the length and shape for your nail extensions, consider your lifestyle. If you are new to extensions, start with a moderate length and shape, such as almond or square. For an active lifestyle, shorter extensions are recommended as they last longer.

3. Hydrate like its Mumbai weather

Just like your skin, your nails need proper hydration to remain strong and healthy. Incorporate cuticle oil into your daily routine; it’s a fantastic way to take care of your nails. Applying a drop or two daily can keep your nails and surrounding skin moisturised, giving your manicure that glossy, salon-fresh appearance.

4. Gloves are your best friends

Consider wearing gloves when tackling chores like doing the dishes, gardening, or any tasks involving dirt. Avoid washing your hands too much, as it can weaken nail glue and cause your extensions to loosen more quickly.

5. Manage stress without biting

We understand that life can be overwhelming, but biting your nail extensions isn’t a solution to stress. This habit can not only damage your nails but also pose health risks, leading to uncomfortable situations at your next salon appointment.

6. Regular refills are essential

As your natural nails grow, they push your extensions away from the cuticle, which can affect both the appearance and strength of the nails. To keep your extensions looking their best, aim to schedule regular refills every 2-3 weeks. Remember, neglecting refills can lead to lifting or breakage, and no one wants to deal with grown-out extensions, so it’s best to stay on top of this!

7. Protect your nails from excessive heat

Nail extensions are designed to be durable, but extreme heat can affect the glue and structure of the materials, leading them to break. To prolong the life of your nails, try to keep them away from hot baths, saunas and while cooking for long periods. Taking these precautions can help maintain the integrity of your nails.
