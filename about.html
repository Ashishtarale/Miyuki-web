Mind, Body and Soul

India's first luxury style lounge

Welcome to MIYUKI, where luxury meets glamour - a world of style and elegance that makes you look stunning, everyday.
From bold, avant-garde creations to timeless sophistication, our celebrity hair stylists, award winning nail artists & technicians and beauty experts bring your vision to life.
Immerse yourself in a realm of extravagant indulgence, boasting one of the largest nail bars, a range of hair styling & mani-pedi stations and a serene beauty spa beckoning you into the depths of reinvigorating bliss.

For those who revel in life's finer treasures, we present the ethereal Velvet Room—a secluded haven where you may luxuriate in our beauty, styling, and grooming services, cloaked in hushed tranquility, attended by your very own entourage of style virtuosos.
At MIYUKI, it’s more than beauty—it’s an unforgettable experience designed just for you. Come, discover your best self with us!
